Terms and Rules for Insightful Home to Home Tutoring Services

1

Registration

Mandatory Registration: All students must complete the registration process prior to attending any sessions. This helps us to understand your educational needs and tailor our services accordingly.

Accurate Information: It is essential to provide accurate and up-to-date information during registration, including contact details and preferred subjects.

2

Payment

Tuition Fees: All fees must be paid in full after the commencement of any tutoring sessions. Payment options will be provided upon registration.

Refund Policy: Refunds for canceled sessions will follow our cancellation policy. Please refer to Section 4 for details.

3

Scheduling

Flexible Scheduling: We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your availability. Please communicate your preferred times during registration.

Session Duration: Each tutoring session typically lasts one hour, but can be adjusted based on mutual agreement.

4

Cancellation and Rescheduling

24-Hour Notice Required: To cancel or reschedule a session without incurring a charge, you must provide at least 24 hours' notice.

Late Cancellations: Cancellations made less than 24 hours before a scheduled session will result in a charge equivalent to the full session fee.

5

Conduct and Behavior

Respectful Environment: All students are expected to maintain a respectful and positive attitude during tutoring sessions. Disruptive behavior will not be tolerated and may lead to dismissal from our services.

Engagement: Active participation is encouraged. Students should come prepared with questions and materials relevant to the session.

6

Confidentiality

Privacy Protection: We are committed to protecting the privacy of our students. All personal information and academic records will be kept confidential and used solely for the purpose of providing educational services.

Sharing of Information: Under no circumstances will we share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

7

Communication

Open Dialogue:Students and parents are encouraged to communicate openly with tutors about any concerns or feedback regarding the tutoring process.

Contact Information: Ensure that you provide current contact information so we can reach you regarding scheduling and updates.

8

Quality Assurance

Feedback Mechanism:We value your feedback and encourage students and parents to share their experiences. This helps us to continuously improve our services.

Tutor Assessment:Regular assessments of tutor performance will be conducted to ensure high standards of education.
